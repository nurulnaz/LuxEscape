The Jerai Hotel

Sungai Petani, Kedah

Hotel Overview

The Jerai Hotel Sungai Petani is a well-established premier business hotel located strategically in the town center of Sungai Petani, Kedah. Designed to fulfill the needs of corporate travelers, leisure seekers, and group events, the hotel provides classic warm hospitality, comprehensive seminar and event spaces, and comfortable urban lodging with excellent connectivity to major transport links and commercial districts.

Facilities & Services

  • Bunga Padi Coffee House (Local & Western Fusion Dining)
  • Grand Ballroom & Comprehensive Seminar Rooms
  • Complimentary High-Speed Wi-Fi Access
  • 24-Hour Front Desk & Concierge Services
  • Ample Secured Private Guest Parking Bays
  • Laundry and Dry Cleaning Services

Review

Pros
  • Prime location in the heart of town, close to KTM train station
  • Extremely spacious meeting facilities and banquet capabilities
  • Highly professional staff and well-maintained room service
Cons
  • Classic architectural aesthetic (some fixtures feature traditional design styles)
  • Can experience high foot traffic during corporate events and wedding weekends
  • No integrated outdoor swimming pool facility on-site
