HOTEL NEO+

Georgetown, Penang

Hotel Overview

Hotel Neo+ Penang is a stylish, 100% smoke-free hotel located in the heart of Georgetown. Managed by Archipelago International, it features contemporary art-deco designs mixed with vibrant city vibes. It is an ideal urban escape for trendseeking business travelers, dynamic families, and couples eager to explore Penang's cultural heritage while enjoying modern, high-tech hotel amenities.

Facilities & Amenities

  • Noodles Cafe (In-house Dining)
  • Rooftop Swimming Pool (with 360-degree city view)
  • Fitness Centre / Gymnasium
  • Multi-purpose Meeting Rooms
  • Free High-Speed Wi-Fi throughout the property
  • 24-Hour Security & Front Desk

Rooms

Room Category Bed Configuration

Neo Room (Double) 1 Queen Bed / 1 King Bed

Review

Pros
  • Superb rooftop pool overlooking the Georgetown cityscape
  • Within short walking distance to KOMTAR and food streets
  • Clean, trendy design with friendly tech-driven room setups
Cons
  • Multi-storey multi-tier parking spaces can feel narrow for large SUVs
  • Peak holiday hours may result in longer elevator waiting times
  • Strictly non-smoking property (may be a con for some guests)
